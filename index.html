Common Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account settings. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be billed the prorated difference immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.
Is there a free trial for the Pro plan?
Yes, every Pro plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You'll be asked for payment details after the trial period ends if you choose to continue.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All transactions are secured via Stripe.
How does team billing work?
Pro plans are billed per user per month. You'll be charged for each seat you provision. You can add or remove seats at any time and the amount will be prorated accordingly.
